# Privacy Policy — Colouring Bookshelf

*Last updated: March 2026*

Colouring Bookshelf is a free app developed by Cryptid Colouring.

---

## Data Collection

Colouring Bookshelf does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no servers, no user accounts, and no internet communication of any kind beyond loading the app itself.

---

## Data Storage

All information you enter — book titles, notes, cover photos, and calendar events — is stored exclusively on your own device using your browser's local storage. This data is never transmitted anywhere and is only accessible to you on your device.

---

## Photos

If you choose to add cover photos to your books, those images are processed and stored entirely on your device. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or accessed by anyone other than you.

---

## Third Parties

Colouring Bookshelf does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

---

## Children

Colouring Bookshelf does not collect any data from any users, including children.

---

## Changes

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect that.
