👋 Getting Started

Your Colouring Bookshelf lives entirely on your device — no account needed, no internet required after the first load. All your books are saved automatically as you go.

➕ Adding a Book

Tap Add Book in the bottom menu. Fill in the title (required) and any other details you know — author, tags, how many pages the book has, and how many you've coloured so far. Tap the photo box to add a cover image from your camera roll. Hit Save Book when you're done.

📚 Your Library

All your books appear here. Tap any book to see its details, mark it as used, or edit it.

Use the Sort buttons to order your library by title, author, or pages coloured.

Use the Filter buttons to narrow down what you see — physical books, digital books, used, unused, or hide books you've fully completed.

🏆😈📅 Special Shelves

To move a book to a special shelf, tap the book → Edit → scroll to the Shelf section → toggle the shelf you want.

🏆 The Honour Shelf is for books you've finished — completed and celebrated.

😈 The Naughty Shelf is for books you've parked — started and abandoned, or ones you're not sure about.

📅 The Seasonal Shelf is for books tied to specific holidays, seasons, or months.

All three shelves appear as collapsible sections at the bottom of your Library once they have books in them.

📊 Tracking Progress

When adding or editing a book, enter the Total Pages in Book and Pages Coloured So Far. The app will calculate and display a progress bar and percentage on each book card automatically.

🔍 Searching

Tap Search in the bottom menu and type anything — the app searches book titles, authors, and tags all at once.

📈 Stats

Tap Stats to see an overview of your collection — total books, how many you've used, physical vs digital breakdown, total pages coloured across all books, and a table of your most collected authors.

📅 Calendar

Tap Calendar to plan your colouring projects. Tap any date to select it, then tap Add event on this day to create an event with a name and optional notes.

You can mark events as ✅ Completed or ❌ Skipped by tapping the event's edit button. Completed events turn green, skipped events turn red — making it easy to scan what's still to do at a glance.

To set a reminder notification, toggle 🔔 Remind me when creating or editing an event, then choose a time and how far in advance you'd like to be notified. The app will need to be opened at least once within 48 hours of the event for the reminder to fire.

Upcoming events appear in a list below the calendar. Events with reminders show a blue 🔔 badge.

📤 Sharing Your List

From the top of the Library screen, tap Share List to send a plain text version of your books via WhatsApp, Messages, email, or any other app on your phone.

Tap Share File to export your full data as a file — useful for sending to another Colouring Bookshelf user so they can import it directly.

🔀 Comparing Lists

Tap Compare Lists at the top of the Library. Load someone else's exported backup file and the app will instantly show which books you have in common and which ones are only in their list.

💾 Backing Up Your Data

Tap the ⚙️ icon in the top right → scroll down to Your Data → tap Export Backup. This saves a file to your phone containing all your books and photos.

To restore after reinstalling: open the app, go to ⚙️ → Your Data → Import Backup, and select your saved file.

The app will remind you to back up if it's been more than 14 days since your last export. We strongly recommend keeping a recent backup somewhere safe — like cloud storage or emailed to yourself.

⚠️ iPhone users especially: Apple can clear app data if your phone runs low on storage. Back up regularly.

🎨 Changing the Colour Theme

Tap the ⚙️ icon in the top right. Choose from one of the preset colour schemes, or tap the colour circles under Custom Colours to open the colour picker.

The colour picker shows a hue ring — drag around it to choose a colour family — and two sliders for Saturation (how vivid the colour is) and Brightness (how light or dark). A preview swatch shows what your selection will look like before you confirm.
